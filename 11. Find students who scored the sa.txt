11. Find students who scored the same marks in a subject within the same class
Tables

student (st_id, name, class)
marks (st_id, sub, marks)

Question
Write a query to find students who have duplicate marks in the same subject within the same class.


With T1 as(
select s.*,m.sub,m.marks from student s Left Join marks m on s.st_id=m.st_id
),
T2 as
(select class,sub,marks,count(*) from T1 Group By class,sub,marks Having count(*)>1
)

select T1.st_id,T1.st_name,T2.* from T2 left join T1 on T2.class=T1.class and T2.sub=T1.sub and T2.marks=T1.marks;
