12. Find students who scored more than 90 in at least two subjects
Tables

student (st_id, name, class)
marks (st_id, sub, marks)

Question
Write a query to list students who scored more than 90 marks in at least two subjects.

st_id name class sub marks


With T1 as
(
select s.*,m.sub,m.marks from student s Left Join marks m on s.st_id=m.st_id where m.marks>90
)

select class,st_id,count(*) from T1 Group By class,st_id having count(*)>=2
