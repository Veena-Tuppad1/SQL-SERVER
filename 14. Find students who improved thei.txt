14. Find students who improved their marks across subjects

(Assume subjects are taken in sequence)

Tables

marks (st_id, sub,sub_order, marks)

Question
Write a query to find students whose marks increased from one subject to the next.



With T1 as(

Select m.*,Lag(marks) Over(Partition by st_id order by sub_order) as prev_marks from marks m
)

Select st_id from T1 Group By st_id Having Min(marks-prev_marks)>0
