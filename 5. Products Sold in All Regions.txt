5. Products Sold in All Regions

Requirement: Find products that were sold in every available region.

sales

| product_id | region |
| ---------: | ------ |
|        301 | North  |
|        301 | South  |
|        301 | East   |
|        302 | North  |
|        302 | South  |

Select product_id,COUNT(DISTINCT region) as count from sales Group By  product_id Having COUNT(DISTINCT region)=(select count(distinct region) from sales)
