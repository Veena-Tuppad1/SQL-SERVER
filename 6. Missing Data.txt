6. Missing Data

Table: Orders

order_id	customer_id	order_date

Question:
Find customers who placed orders in 2024 but not in 2025.


With T1 as
(
  select o.* from Orders o Where Year(order_date)=2024
)

select T1.*  from T1 left join orders o on T1.customer_id=o.customer_id and Year(order_date)=2025 where o.customer_id is null
