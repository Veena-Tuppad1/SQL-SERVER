8. Students who failed in at least two subject (marks < 35)
Tables

student
| st_id | name | class |

marks
| st_id | sub | marks |

Question
Write a query to find students who failed in one or more subjects.


With T1 as(
Select s.*,m.sub,m.marks from student s left join marks m on s.st_id=m.st_id
)


Select st_id,name,count(*) from T1 where marks<35 group by st_id,name having count(*)>=2
